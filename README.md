# AI Digital Clone

**AI Digital Clone** is a tool that allows AI chatbots or large language models (LLMs) to manage and interact through your social media accounts on your behalf. It is designed for users who want to take a break from social platforms without disappearing from conversations or communities — your digital clone maintains interactions while you focus elsewhere.

> ⚠️ Current version supports **Discord only**.

---

## Concept

The idea behind AI Digital Clone is simple:

- You step away from social media
- Your AI clone continues responding and interacting
- Conversations remain active
- Your presence is maintained without manual effort

This enables healthier digital boundaries without social disconnection.

---

- AI-driven conversational responses
